Welcome to DoBu Martial Arts

Since 2003, we have offered a large range of martial arts, private lessons and more. Started by our head martial arts coach Mauricio Gomez we offer lessons in jiu-jitsu, Karate, Judo and Muay Thai. Our state of the art gym offers a large matted martial arts area, a fully equipped gym with a sauna, a steam room as well as changing and shower facilities.
